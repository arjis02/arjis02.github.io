Reinforcing the Positive

July 10, 2015

Looking back at my life, the times where I was the most proud of myself were when I stayed true to these values:
Values Compliance [Disagree(0) - Agree(5)]
Accomplishment 5
Ambition 5
Confidence 5
Health 5
Personal Development 5
Close Relationships 5
Courage 5
Efficiency 5
Music 5
Pleasure 5
Recognition 5
Romance 5
Will-Power 5
I try to uphold these values to the best of my ability. But the one that I believe to be ultimately important is Personal Development. I do not believe this should ever stop. Just as the environment around us evolves, we, as people, should be ready to evolve our thinking, as well. There is always something new out there to learn. And we should always welcome new ideas and innovation.
The last time I was asked for advice, it was in regards to my diet. This relates to the emphasis I put on the value of Health. I make sure I take care of my body so that I continue to feel good. And when you feel good, you can focus on the other values.
As long as I strive to live up to my values, I believe I should be able to overcome any stereotype threats that may reveal itself during my time at DBC. I think about my strengths every day and continue to try improving upon them. I haven't really given meditation a chance, yet. But I do plan on exercising it routinely during the more intense portion of DBC.
